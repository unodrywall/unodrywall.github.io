About Uno Drywall

Building Quality Walls,
One Project at a Time

Uno Drywall Ltd is a full-service drywall and interior systems contractor based in Etobicoke, Ontario. We deliver high-quality drywall solutions for commercial and residential projects throughout the Greater Toronto Area.

From new construction to renovations, our experienced team handles every project with precision, clean workmanship, and respect for your space and timeline. We do the job right the first time.

What We Do

Complete Drywall & Interior Solutions

From framing to finishing, we handle every step of your interior project with expert craftsmanship.

🧱

Drywall Installation

Full board installation for new builds, additions, and renovations. Precise hanging with clean lines.

🔧

Taping & Mudding

Expert joint taping, mudding, and sanding for seamless, paint-ready surfaces.

✨

Drywall Finishing

Level 3, 4, and 5 finishing to match your project requirements. Smooth walls every time.

🔩

Steel Framing

Light-gauge steel stud framing for walls, bulkheads, soffits, and ceiling grids.

🏗️

T-Bar Ceilings

Drop ceiling systems for commercial and residential spaces with acoustical tile options.

🛡️

Soundproofing

Batt, blown-in, and soundproofing insulation for improved comfort and noise reduction.

🔨

Drywall Repairs

Patch jobs, water damage repair, and crack treatment. Invisible repairs that blend perfectly.

🎨

Texture Finishes

Knockdown, orange peel, smooth skim coats, and custom textures for any design vision.
