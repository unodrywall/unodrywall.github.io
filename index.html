About Us

Building Quality Walls, One Project at a Time

Uno Drywall Ltd is a full-service drywall and interior systems contractor based in Etobicoke, Ontario. We specialize in delivering high-quality drywall solutions for both commercial and residential projects across the Greater Toronto Area.

From new construction to renovations, our experienced team handles every project with precision, clean workmanship, and respect for your space and timeline. We take pride in doing the job right the first time.

Our Services

Complete Drywall & Interior Solutions

From framing to finishing, we handle every step of your interior project with expert craftsmanship.

🧱

Drywall Installation

Full drywall board installation for new builds, additions, and renovations. Precise hanging with minimal waste and clean lines.

🔧

Taping & Mudding

Expert joint taping, mudding, and sanding for seamless, paint-ready surfaces. Multiple finish levels available.

✨

Drywall Finishing

Level 3, 4, and 5 finishing to match your project requirements. Smooth, flawless walls every time.

🔩

Steel Framing

Light-gauge steel stud framing for interior walls, bulkheads, soffits, and ceiling grids. Straight, square, and plumb.

🏗️

T-Bar & Suspended Ceilings

Drop ceiling systems for commercial and residential spaces. Clean grid installation with acoustical tile options.

🛡️

Insulation & Soundproofing

Batt, blown-in, and soundproofing insulation for walls and ceilings. Improved energy efficiency and noise reduction.

🔨

Drywall Repairs

Patch jobs, water damage repair, hole fixes, and crack treatment. Invisible repairs that blend with your existing walls.

🎨

Texture & Specialty Finishes

Knockdown, orange peel, smooth skim coats, and custom textures to match any design vision.
