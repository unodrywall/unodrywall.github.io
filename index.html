FAQ

Questions we get asked a lot.

How much does drywall cost per square foot in Toronto?

+
Typically $2.50–$5.00/sqft depending on scope, ceiling height, and finish level. We'll give you an exact number after a free site visit — no guesswork, no surprises.

How long does a typical project take?

+
A standard room: 2–3 days. A full basement: about a week. Commercial projects are scoped individually. We always give you a clear timeline before starting.

Do you work with general contractors?

+
Absolutely. A large portion of our work comes from GCs who sub us on commercial and residential builds. We coordinate with other trades, meet deadlines, and don't hold up your schedule.

Are you licensed and insured?

+
Yes. Full liability insurance and WSIB coverage. We can provide certificates on request for any project.

What areas do you cover?

+
The entire GTA — Toronto, Etobicoke, Mississauga, Brampton, Vaughan, Markham, Richmond Hill, Oakville, and surrounding areas.

What finish levels do you offer?

+
Level 3, 4, and 5. Level 5 is our smoothest finish — ideal for areas with critical lighting. We'll recommend the right level based on your project.

Can you match existing textures?

+
Yes. Knockdown, orange peel, smooth — we match existing textures so repairs blend seamlessly. Most people can't tell where the patch starts and ends.

Do you handle permits?

+
Drywall work itself typically doesn't require a permit in Ontario. If your project involves structural changes, we'll coordinate with your GC or advise on what's needed.
