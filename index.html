Our Services

Complete drywall & interior solutions.

From framing to finishing, we handle every step with expert craftsmanship and premium materials.

Drywall Installation

Full board installation for new builds, additions, and renovations. Precise hanging with clean lines.

Taping & Finishing

Steel Framing

FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

How much does drywall installation cost per square foot?

+
Drywall installation in the GTA typically ranges from $2.50 to $5.00 per square foot depending on the project scope, ceiling height, and finish level. Contact us for a free, detailed estimate tailored to your specific project.

How long does a typical drywall project take?

+
A typical residential room takes 2-3 days for boarding and finishing. Larger commercial projects are scoped individually. We always provide a clear timeline before any work begins.

Do you handle both commercial and residential projects?

+
Yes. We serve residential homeowners, general contractors, and commercial developers across the Greater Toronto Area. No project is too big or too small.

Are you licensed and insured?

+
Absolutely. Uno Drywall Ltd is fully licensed and carries comprehensive liability insurance and WSIB coverage for complete project protection.

What areas do you serve?

+
We serve the entire Greater Toronto Area including Toronto, Etobicoke, Mississauga, Brampton, Vaughan, Markham, Richmond Hill, Oakville, and surrounding communities.

Do you provide free estimates?

+
Yes. We offer free on-site consultations and detailed estimates with transparent pricing. No obligation, no hidden fees. Call us or use the contact form to schedule yours.

What finish levels do you offer?

+
We offer Level 3, Level 4, and Level 5 finishes. Level 5 provides the smoothest, highest-quality surface — ideal for areas with critical lighting. We'll recommend the right level for your project.

Can you match existing wall textures for repairs?

+
Yes. Our team specializes in matching existing textures including knockdown, orange peel, and smooth finishes. Our goal is invisible repairs that blend seamlessly with your existing walls.
